Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our Mystery Box subscriptions.

What comes in each Mystery Box?

Each box contains 3-7 premium 3D printed items depending on your subscription level. Items are selected based on your interest preferences and may include functional gadgets, desk accessories, art pieces, or specialized items.

Can I change my interest preferences?

Yes! You can update your interests at any time from your account dashboard. Changes will apply to your next shipment if made before the monthly cutoff date.

What is the shipping schedule?

Monthly boxes ship on the 10th of each month. Bi-monthly boxes ship on the 10th of every other month starting from your first subscription month.

Are these items exclusive to the Mystery Box?

Yes, most items in our Mystery Boxes are exclusive designs not available elsewhere. We focus on creating unique, high-quality pieces specifically for our subscribers.
