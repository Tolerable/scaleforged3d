About Scale Forged

Scale Forged Manufacturing was founded by a team of engineers and designers passionate about the potential of additive manufacturing to transform how products are designed and made.

With facilities in both the United States and United Kingdom, we serve clients globally with rapid prototyping, production, and custom design services. Our advanced 3D printing technologies and skilled team allow us to deliver exceptional quality with industry-leading turnaround times.

We're committed to pushing the boundaries of what's possible with 3D printing, helping our clients innovate faster and more efficiently than ever before.
